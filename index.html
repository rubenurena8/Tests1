Luck = actual points won minus points “deserved” by the post-match run of play (post-match odds treated as the deserved win/draw/loss split, draw counted as half). Positive = won more than performances merited. Mispricing = how far the market’s pre-match estimate sat below (or above) what the run of play revealed, i.e. post-match minus pre-match win expectancy summed per team. A large positive diamond means the market systematically under-rated the team going in.
