Luck & Mispricing Ledger

Two separate questions the scoreline can't answer. Luck asks whether a team got a better result than its performance earned. Mispricing asks whether the market had the team's quality wrong. Both are measured against the xG‑deserved result — the run of play — not the final score.

Luck — result vs performance

Did you get more than you deserved on the day? Winning a match the run of play said you'd lose is good fortune; being outplayed and still going through is riding variance.

actual result − xG‑deserved result

Mispricing — performance vs price

Was the market wrong about you? Performing above what the pre‑match odds implied isn't luck — it's quality the price missed. This is the underrated‑team signal.

xG‑deserved result − odds‑implied result
